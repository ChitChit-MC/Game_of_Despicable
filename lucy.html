Lucy Wilde

Lucy Wilde is a major character of the Despicable Me franchise. She was a goofy[1] agent of the Anti-Villain League. She is the love interest, later wife of Felonius Gru and adoptive mother to Margo, Edith, and Agnes. She is one of the main characters in the movies Despicable Me 2 and of Despicable Me 3. Beautiful Egypt is also known as her sad theme.
